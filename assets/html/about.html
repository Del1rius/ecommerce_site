About US

Industry Leading Innovators, Alienware, manufactures the best gaming laptops, gaming monitors and gaming desktops.
We are the best in every field that we occupy.

Alienware is an American computer hardware subsidiary of Dell. Their product range is dedicated to gaming computers and can be identified by their alien-themed designs. Alienware was founded in 1996 by Nelson Gonzalez and Alex Aguila, and is currently led by Vivian Lien. The name "Alienware was chosen because of the founders's fondness for the hit television series "The X-Files", which also inspired the science-fiction themed names of product lines such as "Area-51", "Hangar 17" and "Aurora".
